Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?

Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express are accepted at Checkout.

We accept PayPal, but for us to do so, you must contact support@hingeto.com. We cannot guarantee that by the time we respond, that there will be inventory remaining for the item you'd like.

Do you store my credit card information?

No we do not. All information is stored by our processor, Stripe. We only store the last 4 and card type, so that you may easily reuse the same credit card for a future purchase (it is kept as a way for you to easily reidentify the payment method, even though Stripe is the custodian).

I have a question about my order

Please email us at support@hingeto.com and we will respond within 2 - 3 business days.
